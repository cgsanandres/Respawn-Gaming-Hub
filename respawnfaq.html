SUPPORT & FAQ

Find answers to common questions or get help with technical issues – we’re here to keep your gaming experience smooth!

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I book a gaming station? +
You can book stations online via our Pricing & Membership page, or reserve in-person at the hub. Members get priority booking and discounted rates!
What are your membership benefits? +
Members get 15% off hourly rates, priority tournament registration, free gear rentals, exclusive shop discounts, and access to member-only game nights!
Do you offer gaming equipment rentals? +
Yes! We rent out mechanical keyboards, gaming mice, headsets, and controllers for a small hourly fee. Members get free rentals for up to 2 hours per visit.
Can I bring my own gaming gear? +
Absolutely! You’re welcome to bring your own keyboards, mice, or controllers – all stations have extra USB ports and compatibility with most devices.
How do I join tournaments? +
Register online via our Events page, or sign up in-person at the hub. Entry fees vary by tournament – members get 50% off registration!

COMMON ISSUES & FIXES

NETWORK CONNECTION PROBLEMS

  • Check if your device is connected to "RespawnHub_WiFi" (password: respawngame)
  • Restart your game or device to refresh the connection
  • Move closer to access points if on WiFi – dead zones exist near the back wall
  • Wired Ethernet ports are available at all stations for better stability

HEADSET/ AUDIO ISSUES

  • Ensure 3.5mm jack is fully plugged in or USB is connected securely
  • Adjust volume levels in both your game and hub station settings
  • Test with a different headset at the station to rule out hardware issues
  • Ask staff for a replacement if provided gear is faulty

PC/ CONSOLE PERFORMANCE

  • Close unused background apps to free up system resources
  • Lower in-game graphics settings if experiencing lag
  • Ask staff to restart the station if problems persist
  • Tournament stations have priority performance tuning – book in advance!

ACCOUNT/ LOGIN ISSUES

  • Reset your password via the Login page
  • Ensure you’re using the same email used to create your account
  • Clear browser cache/cookies if having trouble accessing member features
  • Contact support to merge duplicate accounts
